Transport Engineering · Sustainable Mobility · EV Modelling

Suhail Akhtar

I am a Research Fellow at Trinity College Dublin working on sustainable transport, transport behaviour change, electric vehicle adoption, mobility hubs, modal shift, and emissions reduction. My research focuses on the construction of representative electric vehicle drive cycles, speed-profile modelling, electric vehicle energy analysis, and data-driven approaches for transport planning and infrastructure assessment.

My work combines transport engineering, machine learning, road-network analysis, traffic simulation, and environmental datasets to support evidence-based mobility planning. I have worked on Horizon Europe and SEAI-funded research involving electric vehicle drive cycles, charging needs, low-carbon transport interventions, and transport decarbonisation in Ireland and Europe.
